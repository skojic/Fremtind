Developing Bold 360 AI

Last updated: June 25th, 2018

Knowledgebase Articles

Bold 360 Artificial Inteligence, or as we like to call it "robot" is powered by large knowledgebase containing varius "Question - Answer" articles. There are three types of articles created, the ones with the straight answer, so called FAQ's, the ones with the branching options where user must provide some choice to get the final answer. The last type of article are the special one's. Those articles are not containing any relevant information for the customers. They provide code for the developers, for third party apps like Purecloud live agent chat to work with Bold 360 without problems.
