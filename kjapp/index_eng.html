Creating acount

You can create your user account with the Facebook account, or by email. After you login you can choose from the settings menu car and payment options. There you can add as much as you want, creadit cards and vehicles. Everything is simple, intuitive and easy.

Parking your car

After adding the vehicle and payment option. GPS location of yours is showing nearest parking zones. you have to choose one, and click on P logo. Parking section will be opened, and you can choose options for parking. intuitive slider will determine the duration, and slide to park operation will begin your session.

Environment contribution

We care about earth, nature, recycling and fight against pollution, donating small portion of parking prices to the "In The Same Boat" people, to support fight against litter and pollution.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I start using the app?

Just download the app from App Store or Play Store, create user using Facebook or using email address and you're halfway thru. Just add your vehicle and payment options and you are fully operational.

How do I add my vehicle?

After login go to the main menu, there will be vehicle section where you can add your vehicle. Other way is to choose your parking zone first, then you can add your vehicle from parking drawer menu, before you start parking.

How can I add payment option?

You can add payment option by going to the main menu and choosing Payment Methods. Other way is, when you choose your parking spot and enter the parking drawer menu, before you start parking you can add payment option. Payment options that we support are: Visa, Mastercard, American Express.

What happens if I turn off KJAPP in the middle of the parking?

If you accidentally turn off KJAPP in the middle of the parking session, when you open KJAPP again the session will be still in progress, and offer you option to turn it off. If you forgot your phone somewhere and you have your parking session active, you can still log in with your user credentials on another phone and session in progress would be replicated on that phone, where you can turn off, or extend or do whatever you like with it.

What's with that environmental reference?

We care about Norway, we care about environment, that's why in partnership with people from "In The Same Boat" we decided to donate piece of your parking costs to them in order to help them fight against pollution of our coasts.

Can I use KJAPP without adding payment and vehicle options?

Yes you can! When you launch KJAPP you have guest mode option, where you can see all of our available option and try to use app without actual parking. In order to park you would have to register and add vehicle and payment option.

Are you keeping my sensitive information about cards and payments.

No, we care about our user privacy. We are using stripe as our payment solution provider, and every session that goes thru our app, is secure and stored on their server according to GDPR and PCI DSS compliance rules.

What do I do if I have some problems with charging or application?

Every working day from 8:00 - 20:00 there are technical support people more than happy to reply on all your questions, or emails. You can contact them on the details provided in Technical Support page
